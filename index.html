About me

Engineer, builder, lifelong learner

I work on the product team for a B2B software solution for space management and event planning, based in Austin, TX. I studied at The University of Texas, starting in Petroleum Engineering before finding my way into software and product — a pivot I'm very happy to have made.

My strengths sit at the intersection of the technical and the human: translating user pain points into things engineers can build, and keeping teams aligned on the why. It's a mix I've sharpened through graduate research, project management, and work experience. Some of the areas that interest me include software, data, energy, and finance.

What keeps me engaged is building products that feel effortless to use. I strive to create the ultimate user experience. One thing about me is certain — I never stop learning.

My hobbies include playing tennis, working out, traveling, and cooking. I enjoy a good challenge and love trying new things.
